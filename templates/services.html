Give Details Of The Person

  1. 1) If someone you know is missing or if you located/found a missing person in your locality or anywhere else then you should use the Upload feature of our missing person finder to upload the details along with a video or image of the missing person.


  2. 2) We will store the details in our database so it can be used by the loved ones of the missing person while locating him/her.


  3. 3) The Upload feature will extract the face from those image scans and will save it with the details (eg Name, Age, Father/Mother Name, current location etc) of the missing person.

Find The Missing Person

  1. 1) If the record of the missing person your are looking for is available in our database then our Match Face feature will provide you the details of the person, for that you have to show a video or image of the missing person to our finder.


  2. 2) The Face recognition system will use the video/image shown to the finder to find a possible match from our database, if match is found the system will diaplay those details.


  3. 3) If unfortunately the details of the missing person is not in our database, then the finder will show that the person is unknown to it. In this case we urge you to use our upload feature.

About Us

Our platform is an application where individuals can scan and upload data of any person who they believe are missing.

Our product will extract the face from those image scans and will save it with the data (eg Name, Age, Father/Mother Name, current location etc) of the missing person.
